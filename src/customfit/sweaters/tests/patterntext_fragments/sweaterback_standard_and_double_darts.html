Sweater Back

CO 98 stitches using long tail cast-on or other cast-on method of your choice. Work even in 1x1 Ribbing for 1½"/4 cm (10 rows from beginning), ending with a WS row.

Switch to Stockinette.

Work even until piece measures 3"/7.5 cm, ending with a RS row: 19 rows from beginning.

Marker placement row (WS): Work 9 stitches, pm of color B, work 11 stitches, pm of color A, work 58 stitches, pm of color A, work 11 stitches, pm of color B, work 9 stitches to end. Four markers of two colors placed for waist shaping.

Decrease Row One (RS): Work to 2 stitches before second marker (color A), ssk, sm, work to third marker (color A), sm, k2tog, work to end. Two stitches decreased. Work 3 rows even.

Then, work Decrease Row One once more. Work 1 row even.

Decrease Row Two (RS): Work to 2 stitches before first marker (color B), ssk, sm, work to fourth marker (color B), sm, k2tog, work to end. Two stitches decreased. Work 1 row even.

Then, work Decrease Row One. Work 1 row even.

Then, alternate Decrease Rows Two and One every RS row 3 additional times.

10 total decrease rows worked since beginning. 78 stitches remain.

Work even as established until piece measures 7½"/19 cm, ending with a WS row: 52 rows from beginning, 7 rows from (and including) decrease row.

Increase Row One (RS): Work to second marker (color A), m1R, sm, work to third marker (color A), sm, m1L, work to end. Two stitches increased. Work 3 rows even.

Work Increase Row One once more. Work 1 row even.

Repeat the following 4 rows a total of 4 times:

Increase Row Two (RS): Work to first marker (color B), m1R, sm, work to fourth marker (color B), sm, m1L, work to end. Two stitches increased. Work 1 row even.

Work Increase row One. Work 1 row even.

When all shaping is complete, 10 total increase rows worked since waist.

Remove all shaping markers.

98 stitches total.

Continue as established until piece measures 16"/40.5 cm, ending with a WS row: 112 rows from beginning, 35 rows from (and including) last increase row.

Shape Armhole:

BO 5 stitches at the beginning of the next 2 rows. BO 3 stitches at the beginning of following 2 rows. Decrease 1 stitch at each end of every RS row 7 times as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Knit 1, ssk, work to last 3 sts, k2tog, k 1. Two stitches decreased.

70 stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 23"/58.5 cm, ending with a WS row: 162 rows from beginning, 34 rows from (and including) last armhole shaping row.

Shape neck:

Next row (RS): Work 19 stitches, BO 32 stitches, work to end. You will now work the left shoulder only; place stitches for right shoulder on a holder if desired.

Work as established, decreasing 1 stitch at neck edge every RS row twice as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Knit 1, ssk, work to end.

When all shaping is complete, 17 shoulder stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 24"/61 cm, ending with a RS row: 169 rows from beginning, 40 rows from (and including) last armhole decrease row.

Shape shoulders:

Next row (WS): BO 9 sts, work to end. Work 1 RS row even.

BO remaining 8 sts.

Reattach yarn to WS of held stitches.

Work as established, decreasing 1 stitch at neck edge every RS row twice as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Work to last 3 sts, k2tog, k 1.

When all shaping is complete, 17 shoulder stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 24"/61 cm, ending with a WS row: 168 rows from beginning, 39 rows from (and including) last armhole decrease row.

Shape shoulders:

Next row (RS): BO 9 sts, work to end. Work 1 WS row even.

BO remaining 8 sts.
