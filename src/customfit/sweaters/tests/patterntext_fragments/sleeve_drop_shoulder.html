Sleeve

CO 45 stitches using long tail or other cast-on method of your choice. Work even in 1x1 Ribbing for ½"/1.5 cm (4 rows), ending with a WS row.

Switch to Sugar Cube Stitch.

Continue as established until sleeve measures ¾"/2 cm, ending with a WS row: 6 rows from beginning.

Shape sleeve:

Work a shaping row every 5 rows 13 times, then every 4 rows 12 times. 25 total shaping rows worked.

The shaping rows are worked as follows:

Increase row (RS): Work 1, m1R, work to last stitch, m1L, work 1. Two stitches increased.

Increase row (WS): Work 1, m1p, work to last stitch, m1p, work 1. Two stitches increased.

95 stitches total.

Work as established until sleeve measures 17½"/44.5 cm, ending with a WS row: 8 rows from (and including) last shaping row, 122 rows from beginning.

BO all stitches.

Actual measurements:

  • Sleeve CO: 9"/23 cm
  • Biceps: 19"/48.5 cm
  • Total sleeve length: 17½"/44.5 cm
