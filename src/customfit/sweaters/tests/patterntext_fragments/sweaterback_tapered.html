Sweater Back

CO 95 stitches using long tail cast-on or other cast-on method of your choice. Work even in 1x1 Ribbing for 1½"/4 cm (10 rows from beginning), ending with a WS row.

Switch to Stockinette.

Continue until piece measures 2¼"/6 cm, ending with a WS row: 15 rows from beginning.

Shape body:

Increase row (RS): Knit 1, m1, work to last stitch, m1, knit 1. Two stitches increased.

Work Increase row every 28 rows 4 times total: 103 stitches for bust.

Continue as established until piece measures 16"/40.5 cm, ending with a WS row: 112 rows from beginning, 13 rows from (and including) last increase row.

Shape Armhole:

BO 5 stitches at the beginning of the next 2 rows. BO 4 stitches at the beginning of following 2 rows. Decrease 1 stitch at each end of every RS row 7 times as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Knit 1, ssk, work to last 3 sts, k2tog, k 1. Two stitches decreased.

71 stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 23"/58.5 cm, ending with a WS row: 162 rows from beginning, 34 rows from (and including) last armhole shaping row.

Shape neck:

Next row (RS): Work 20 stitches, BO 31 stitches, work to end. You will now work the left shoulder only; place stitches for right shoulder on a holder if desired.

Work as established, decreasing 1 stitch at neck edge every RS row twice as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Knit 1, ssk, work to end.

When all shaping is complete, 18 shoulder stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 24"/61 cm, ending with a RS row: 169 rows from beginning, 40 rows from (and including) last armhole decrease row.

Shape shoulders:

Next row (WS): BO 9 sts, work to end. Work 1 RS row even.

BO remaining 9 sts.

Reattach yarn to WS of held stitches.

Work as established, decreasing 1 stitch at neck edge every RS row twice as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Work to last 3 sts, k2tog, k 1.

When all shaping is complete, 18 shoulder stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 24"/61 cm, ending with a WS row: 168 rows from beginning, 39 rows from (and including) last armhole decrease row.

Shape shoulders:

Next row (RS): BO 9 sts, work to end. Work 1 WS row even.

BO remaining 9 sts.

Actual measurements

  • Hip: 19"/48.5 cm
  • Bust/chest: 20½"/52.5 cm
  • Cross-chest: 14¼"/36 cm
  • Shoulder width (per side): 3½"/9 cm
  • Hem to armhole: 16"/40.5 cm
  • Armhole depth: 8"/20.5 cm
  • Hem to shoulder: 24"/61 cm
  • Neck depth (exclusive of trim): 1"/2.5 cm
