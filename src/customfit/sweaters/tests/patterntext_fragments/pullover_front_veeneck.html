Sweater Front

Before you begin, please note that some instructions will overlap. In particular, Armhole shaping will overlap Neckline. Armhole shaping begins on row 113 and ends on row 129. Neckline begins on row 121 and ends on row 159. Please read ahead.

CO 98 stitches using long tail cast-on or other cast-on method of your choice. Work even in 1x1 Ribbing for 1½"/4 cm (10 rows from beginning), ending with a WS row.

Switch to Other Stitch.

Work even until piece measures 3"/7.5 cm, ending with a RS row: 19 rows from beginning.

Marker placement row (WS): Work 25 stitches, pm, work 48 stitches, pm, work 25 stitches to end.

Shape waist:

Decrease row (RS): Work to 2 stitches before first marker, ssk, sm, work to second marker, sm, k2tog, work to end. Two stitches decreased.

Repeat decrease row every 6 rows 4 additional times: 5 total decrease rows worked. 88 stitches remain.

Work even as established until piece measures 7½"/19 cm, ending with a WS row: 52 rows from beginning, 8 rows from (and including) decrease row.

Increase row (RS): Work to first marker, m1R, sm, work to second marker, sm, m1L, work to end. Two stitches increased.

Repeat Increase row every 8 rows 6 more times: 7 total increase rows worked.

Remove all shaping markers.

102 stitches total.

Continue as established until piece measures 16"/40.5 cm, ending with a WS row: 112 rows from beginning, 12 rows from (and including) last increase row.

Shape Armhole:

BO 5 stitches at the beginning of the next 2 rows. BO 3 stitches at the beginning of following 2 rows. Decrease 1 stitch at each end of every RS row 7 times as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Knit 1, ssk, work to last 3 sts, k2tog, k 1. Two stitches decreased.

72 stitches remain.

At the same time:

Continue as established until piece measures 17"/43 cm, ending with a WS row: 120 rows from beginning, 8 rows from start of armhole bind-offs.

Shape neck:

Next row (RS): Work to the center of your stitches, place these stitches on a holder for left shoulder, work to end. You will now work on right shoulder stitches only.

Decrease 1 stitch at neck edge of next RS row and then every 2 rows 18 additional times as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Knit 1, ssk, work to end.

19 total decrease rows worked.

When all shaping is complete, 17 shoulder stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 24"/61 cm, ending with a RS row: 169 rows from beginning, 40 rows from (and including) last armhole decrease row.

Shape shoulders:

Next row (WS): BO 9 sts, work to end. Work 1 RS row even.

BO remaining 8 sts.

Reattach yarn to WS of held stitches.

Decrease 1 stitch at neck edge of next RS row and then every 2 rows 18 additional times as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Work to last 3 sts, k2tog, k 1.

19 total decrease rows worked.

When all shaping is complete, 17 shoulder stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 24"/61 cm, ending with a WS row: 168 rows from beginning, 39 rows from (and including) last armhole decrease row.

Shape shoulders:

Next row (RS): BO 9 sts, work to end. Work 1 WS row even.

BO remaining 8 sts.
