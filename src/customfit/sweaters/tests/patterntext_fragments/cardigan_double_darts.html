Right Front

Before you begin, please note that some instructions will overlap. In particular, Armhole shaping will overlap Neckline. Armhole shaping begins on row 114 and ends on row 129. Neckline begins on row 127 and ends on row 163. Please read ahead.

CO 49 stitches using long tail cast-on or other cast-on method of your choice. Work even in 1x1 Ribbing for 1½"/4 cm (10 rows from beginning), ending with a WS row.

Switch to Other Stitch.

Work even until piece measures 3"/7.5 cm, ending with a RS row: 19 rows from beginning.

Marker placement row (WS): Work 10 stitches, pm of color B, work 15 stitches, pm of color A, work 24 sts to end. Two markers of two colors placed for waist shaping.

Decrease Row One (RS): K to first marker, sm, k2tog, k to end. One stitch decreased. Work 3 rows even.

Then, work Decrease Row One once more. Work 1 row even.

Decrease Row Two (RS): K to second marker, sm, k2tog, k to end. One stitch decreased. Work 1 row even.

Then, work Decrease Row One. Work 1 row even.

Then, alternate Decrease Rows Two and One every RS row 3 additional times.

10 total decrease rows worked since beginning. 39 stitches remain.

Work even as established until piece measures 7½"/19 cm, ending with a WS row: 52 rows from beginning, 7 rows from (and including) decrease row.

Increase Row One (RS): K to first marker, sm, m1L, k to end. One stitch increased. Work 3 rows even.

Work Increase Row One once more. Work 1 row even.

Repeat the following 4 rows a total of 4 times:

Increase Row Two (RS): K to second marker, sm, m1L, k to end. One stitch increased. Work 1 row even.

Work Increase row One. Work 1 row even.

When all shaping is complete, 10 total increase rows worked since waist.

Remove all shaping markers.

49 stitches total.

Continue as established until piece measures 16"/40.5 cm, ending with a RS row: 113 rows from beginning, 36 rows from (and including) last increase row.

Shape Armhole:

Next row (WS): BO 5 stitches, then work as established to end. Work 1 RS row even.

Next row (WS): BO 3 stitches, work to end.

Decrease 1 stitch at armhole edge of every RS row 7 times as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Work to last 3 sts, k2tog, k 1. One stitch decreased.

At the same time:

Continue as established until piece measures 18"/45.5 cm, ending with a WS row: 126 rows from beginning, 13 rows from start of armhole bind-offs.

Shape neck:

Beginning with a RS row, decrease 1 stitch at neck edge of this row and then every 2 rows 18 additional times as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Knit 1, ssk, work to end.

19 total decrease rows worked.

When all shaping is complete, 17 shoulder stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 24"/61 cm, ending with a RS row: 169 rows from beginning, 40 rows from (and including) last armhole decrease row.

Shape shoulders:

Next row (WS): BO 9 sts, work to end. Work 1 RS row even.

BO remaining 8 sts.

Actual measurements

  • Hip: 9¾"/25 cm
  • Waist: 7¾"/20 cm
  • Bust/chest: 9¾"/25 cm
  • Shoulder width (per side): 3½"/8.5 cm
  • Hem to waist: 7½"/19 cm
  • Hem to armhole: 16"/40.5 cm
  • Armhole depth: 8"/20.5 cm
  • Hem to shoulder: 24"/61 cm
  • Neck depth (exclusive of trim): 6"/15 cm
