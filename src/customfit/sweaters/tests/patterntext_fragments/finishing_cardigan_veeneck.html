Finishing Instructions

Wash according to the instructions for the yarn, and dry in the manner you intend to dry the garment. Join shoulder seams using mattress stitch.

Set in sleeves and sew using mattress stitch. Sew side and underarm seams using mattress stitch.

Button-band and Neckline Trim:

Beginning at hem of Right Front, pick up and knit around entire button-band and neckline edge as follows:

Stitches should be picked up and knit at the rate of 1 stitch for every bound-off stitch, 3 out of every 4 stitches along the diagonal, and 2 out of every 3 stitches along vertical sections. Place markers while picking up stitches as follows:

Pick up approximately 90 stitches along right front button band edge, place marker; pick up approximately 116 along neck edge, place marker; pick up approximately 90 along left front edge. Approximately 296 stitches; this exact number is not crucial, but should respect stitch counts for your stitch pattern.

Work even in 1x1 Ribbing for Â½"/1.5 cm (approximately 4 rows), ending with a WS row.

Buttonhole row (RS): Work 5 stitches, *k2tog, yo, k 11 sts, rep from * 5 times, [k2tog, yo] once more, work to first marker, slip first marker, then work to end. 7 buttonholes spaced evenly across right front.

Note: buttonhole instructions should be modified if you did not pick up the specified number of stitches along the right front button band edge.

Work as established in 1x1 Ribbing until trim measures 1"/2.5 cm (approximately 7 rows). BO all stitches.

Weave in all ends.
