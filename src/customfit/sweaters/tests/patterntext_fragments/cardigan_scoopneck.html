Right Front

Before you begin, please note that some instructions will overlap. In particular, Armhole shaping will overlap Neckline. Armhole shaping begins on row 114 and ends on row 129. Neckline begins on row 127 and ends on row 147. Please read ahead.

CO 47 stitches using long tail cast-on or other cast-on method of your choice. Work even in 1x1 Ribbing for 1½"/4 cm (10 rows from beginning), ending with a WS row.

Switch to Other Stitch.

Work even until piece measures 3"/7.5 cm, ending with a RS row: 19 rows from beginning.

Marker placement row (WS): Work 25 stitches, pm, work 22 stitches to end.

Shape waist:

Decrease row (RS): K to marker, sm, k2tog, k to end. One stitch decreased.

Repeat decrease row every 6 rows 4 additional times: 5 total decrease rows worked. 42 stitches remain.

Work even as established until piece measures 7½"/19 cm, ending with a WS row: 52 rows from beginning, 8 rows from (and including) decrease row.

Increase row (RS): Knit to marker, sm, m1L, k to end. One stitch increased.

Repeat Increase row every 8 rows 6 more times: 7 total increase rows worked.

Remove all shaping markers.

49 stitches total.

Continue as established until piece measures 16"/40.5 cm, ending with a RS row: 113 rows from beginning, 13 rows from (and including) last increase row.

Shape Armhole:

Next row (WS): BO 5 stitches, then work as established to end. Work 1 RS row even.

Next row (WS): BO 3 stitches, work to end.

Decrease 1 stitch at armhole edge of every RS row 7 times as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Work to last 3 sts, k2tog, k 1. One stitch decreased.

At the same time:

Continue as established until piece measures 18"/45.5 cm, ending with a WS row: 126 rows from beginning, 13 rows from start of armhole bind-offs.

Shape neck:

Next row (RS): BO 6 stitches, work to end.

Decrease 1 stitch at neck edge of every row 5 times, then every RS row 3 times, and then every other RS row 3 times as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Knit 1, ssk, work to end.

Decrease row (WS): Work to last 3 sts, p2tog-tbl, p 1.

When all shaping is complete, 17 shoulder stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 24"/61 cm, ending with a RS row: 169 rows from beginning, 40 rows from (and including) last armhole decrease row.

Shape shoulders:

Next row (WS): BO 9 sts, work to end. Work 1 RS row even.

BO remaining 8 sts.

Actual measurements

  • Hip: 9½"/24 cm
  • Waist: 8½"/21.5 cm
  • Bust/chest: 9¾"/25 cm
  • Shoulder width (per side): 3½"/8.5 cm
  • Hem to waist: 7½"/19 cm
  • Hem to armhole: 16"/40.5 cm
  • Armhole depth: 8"/20.5 cm
  • Hem to shoulder: 24"/61 cm
  • Neck depth (exclusive of trim): 6"/15 cm
