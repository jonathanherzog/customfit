Finishing Instructions

Wash according to the instructions for the yarn, and dry in the manner you intend to dry the garment. Join shoulder seams using mattress stitch.

Set in sleeves and sew using mattress stitch. Sew side and underarm seams using mattress stitch.

Trim Neckline:

Beginning at right neck edge, pick up and knit stitches around the neckline at the following rates: 1 stitch for every bound-off stitch, 3 out of every 4 stitches along the diagonal, and 2 out of every 3 stitches along vertical sections. Approximately 118 stitches picked up total. (This exact number is not crucial, but should respect stitch counts for your stitch pattern.)

Join for working in the round. Work in 1x1 Ribbing for 1"/2.5 cm (7 rounds), working a double decrease at center neck point every other round. BO all sts.

Weave in all ends.
