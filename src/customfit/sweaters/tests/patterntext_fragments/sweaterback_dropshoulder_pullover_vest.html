Sweater Back

CO 115 stitches using long tail cast-on or other cast-on method of your choice. Work even in 1x1 Ribbing for 1½"/4 cm (10 rows from beginning), ending with a WS row.

Switch to Stockinette.

Work even until piece measures 2"/5.5 cm, ending with a RS row: 13 rows from beginning.

Marker placement row (WS): Work 38 stitches, pm, work 39 stitches, pm, work 38 stitches to end.

Shape waist:

Decrease row (RS): Work to 2 stitches before first marker, ssk, sm, work to second marker, sm, k2tog, work to end. Two stitches decreased.

Repeat decrease row every 6 rows 5 additional times: 6 total decrease rows worked. 103 stitches remain.

Work even as established until piece measures 7½"/19 cm, ending with a WS row: 52 rows from beginning, 8 rows from (and including) decrease row.

Increase row (RS): Work to first marker, m1R, sm, work to second marker, sm, m1L, work to end. Two stitches increased.

Repeat Increase row every 12 rows 3 more times: 4 total increase rows worked.

Remove all shaping markers.

111 stitches total.

Continue as established until piece measures 14½"/37 cm, ending with a WS row: 102 rows from beginning, 14 rows from (and including) last increase row.

Shape Armhole:

BO 3 stitches at the beginning of the next 2 rows.

105 stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 23"/58.5 cm, ending with a WS row: 162 rows from beginning, 59 rows from (and including) last armhole shaping row.

Shape neck:

Next row (RS): Work 37 stitches, BO 31 stitches, work to end. You will now work the left shoulder only; place stitches for right shoulder on a holder if desired.

Work as established, decreasing 1 stitch at neck edge every RS row twice as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Knit 1, ssk, work to end.

When all shaping is complete, 35 shoulder stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 24"/61 cm, ending with a RS row: 169 rows from beginning, 65 rows from (and including) last armhole decrease row.

Shape shoulders:

Next row (WS): BO 18 sts, work to end. Work 1 RS row even.

BO remaining 17 sts.

Reattach yarn to WS of held stitches.

Work as established, decreasing 1 stitch at neck edge every RS row twice as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Work to last 3 sts, k2tog, k 1.

When all shaping is complete, 35 shoulder stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 24"/61 cm, ending with a WS row: 168 rows from beginning, 64 rows from (and including) last armhole decrease row.

Shape shoulders:

Next row (RS): BO 18 sts, work to end. Work 1 WS row even.

BO remaining 17 sts.
