Sleeve

CO 80 stitches using long tail or other cast-on method of your choice. Work even in 1x1 Ribbing for ½"/1.5 cm (4 rows), ending with a WS row.

Switch to Sugar Cube Stitch.

Continue as established until sleeve measures ¾"/2 cm, ending with a WS row: 6 rows from beginning.

Shape sleeve:

Work a shaping row every 20 rows 6 times.

The shaping rows are worked as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Work 1, ssk, work to last 3 stitches, k2tog, work 1. Two stitches decreased.

68 stitches remain.

Work as established until sleeve measures 17½"/44.5 cm, ending with a WS row: 16 rows from (and including) last shaping row, 122 rows from beginning.

Shape sleeve cap:

BO 5 stitches at the beginning of the next 2 rows.

BO 3 stitches at the beginning of the following 2 rows. 52 stitches remain.

Decrease 1 stitch at each end of every 3rd RS row 1 times, then every RS row 15 times as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Knit 1, ssk, work to last 3 sts, k2tog, k 1. Two stitches decreased.

20 stitches remain.

BO 2 stitches at the beginning of the next 4 rows. 12 stitches remain.

BO all stitches.

Actual measurements:

  • Sleeve CO: 16"/40.5 cm
  • Biceps: 13½"/34.5 cm
  • Sleeve CO to sleeve-caps: 17½"/44.5 cm
  • Cap heights: 6½"/16.5 cm
  • Total sleeve length: 24"/61 cm
