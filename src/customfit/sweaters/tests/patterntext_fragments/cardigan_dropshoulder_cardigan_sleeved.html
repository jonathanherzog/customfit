Right Front

CO 58 stitches using long tail cast-on or other cast-on method of your choice. Work even in 1x1 Ribbing for 1½"/4 cm (10 rows from beginning), ending with a WS row.

Switch to Other Stitch.

Work even until piece measures 2"/5.5 cm, ending with a RS row: 13 rows from beginning.

Marker placement row (WS): Work 29 stitches, pm, work 29 stitches to end.

Shape waist:

Decrease row (RS): K to marker, sm, k2tog, k to end. One stitch decreased.

Repeat decrease row every 6 rows 5 additional times: 6 total decrease rows worked. 52 stitches remain.

Work even as established until piece measures 7½"/19 cm, ending with a WS row: 52 rows from beginning, 8 rows from (and including) decrease row.

Increase row (RS): Knit to marker, sm, m1L, k to end. One stitch increased.

Repeat Increase row every 12 rows 3 more times: 4 total increase rows worked.

Remove all shaping markers.

56 stitches total.

Continue as established until piece measures 14½"/37 cm, ending with a RS row: 103 rows from beginning, 15 rows from (and including) last increase row.

Shape Armhole:

Next row (WS): BO 3 stitches, then work as established to end. Work 1 RS row even.

Continue as established until piece measures 18"/45.5 cm, ending with a WS row: 126 rows from beginning, 23 rows from (and including) last armhole shaping row.

Shape neck:

Beginning with a RS row, decrease 1 stitch at neck edge of this row and then every 2 rows 17 additional times as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Knit 1, ssk, work to end.

18 total decrease rows worked.

When all shaping is complete, 35 shoulder stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 24"/61 cm, ending with a RS row: 169 rows from beginning, 65 rows from (and including) last armhole decrease row.

Shape shoulders:

Next row (WS): BO 18 sts, work to end. Work 1 RS row even.

BO remaining 17 sts.

Actual measurements

  • Hip: 11½"/29.5 cm
  • Waist: 10½"/26.5 cm
  • Bust/chest: 11¼"/28.5 cm
  • Shoulder width (per side): 7"/18 cm
  • Hem to waist: 7½"/19 cm
  • Hem to armhole: 14½"/37 cm
  • Armhole depth: 9½"/24 cm
  • Hem to shoulder: 24"/61 cm
  • Neck depth (exclusive of trim): 6"/15 cm

Left Front

CO 58 stitches using long tail cast-on or other cast-on method of your choice. Work even in 1x1 Ribbing for 1½"/4 cm (10 rows from beginning), ending with a WS row.

Switch to Other Stitch.

Work even until piece measures 2"/5.5 cm, ending with a RS row: 13 rows from beginning.

Marker placement row (WS): Work 29 stitches, pm, work 29 stitches to end.

Shape waist:

Decrease row (RS): K to 2 sts before marker, SSK, sm, K to end. One stitch decreased.

Repeat decrease row every 6 rows 5 additional times: 6 total decrease rows worked. 52 stitches remain.

Work even as established until piece measures 7½"/19 cm, ending with a WS row: 52 rows from beginning, 8 rows from (and including) decrease row.

Increase row (RS): Knit to marker, m1R, sm, k to end. One stitch increased.

Repeat Increase row every 12 rows 3 more times: 4 total increase rows worked.

Remove all shaping markers.

56 stitches total.

Continue as established until piece measures 14½"/37 cm, ending with a WS row: 102 rows from beginning, 14 rows from (and including) last increase row.

Shape Armhole:

Next row (RS): BO 3 stitches, then work as established to end. Work 1 WS row even.

Continue as established until piece measures 18"/45.5 cm, ending with a RS row: 127 rows from beginning, 25 rows from (and including) last armhole shaping row.

Work one WS row even.

Shape neck:

Beginning with a RS row, decrease 1 stitch at neck edge of this row and then every 2 rows 17 additional times as follows:

Decrease row (RS): Work to last 3 sts, k2tog, k 1.

18 total decrease rows worked.

When all shaping is complete, 35 shoulder stitches remain.

Continue as established until piece measures 24"/61 cm, ending with a WS row: 168 rows from beginning, 65 rows from (and including) last armhole decrease row.

Shape shoulders:

Next row (RS): BO 18 sts, work to end. Work 1 WS row even.

BO remaining 17 sts.

  • Waist: 10½"/26.5 cm

  • Shoulder width (per side): 7"/18 cm
