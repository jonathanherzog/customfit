Finishing Instructions

Wash according to the instructions for the yarn, and dry in the manner you intend to dry the garment. Join shoulder seams using mattress stitch.

Set in sleeves and sew using mattress stitch. Sew side and underarm seams using mattress stitch.

Trim Neckline:

Beginning at right neck edge, pick up and knit stitches around the neckline at the following rates: 1 stitch for every bound-off stitch, 3 out of every 4 stitches along the diagonal, and 2 out of every 3 stitches along vertical sections. Approximately 140 stitches picked up total. (This exact number is not crucial, but should respect stitch counts for your stitch pattern.)

Work in 1x1 Ribbing for 1"/2.5 cm (7 rows). BO all sts.

Right Front Button-band:

Beginning at hem of Right Front, pick up and knit button-band as follows: Pick up and knit stitches at the rate of 2 for every 3 rows along button band edge from hem to neck, approximately 90 stitches. (This number is not crucial, but should respect stitch counts for your stitch pattern. Buttonhole instructions should be modified if you do not achieve this exact number.)

Buttonhole row (RS): Work 5 stitches, *k2tog, yo, work 11 sts, rep from * 5 more times, [k2tog, yo] once more, work to end. 7 buttonholes spaced evenly across front. (Note: If you prefer a different buttonhole than an eyelet buttonhole, please feel free to substitute it.)

Work even in 1x1 Ribbing until button band measures a total of 1"/2.5 cm (7 rows). BO all stitches.

Left Front Button-band:

Beginning at neck of Left Front, pick up and knit button-band as follows: Pick up and knit stitches at the rate of 2 for every 3 rows along button band edge from neck to hem, approximately 90 stitches. (This number is not crucial, but should respect stitch counts for your stitch pattern. Buttonhole instructions should be modified if you do not achieve this exact number.)

Work even in 1x1 Ribbing for 1"/2.5 cm (approximately 7 rows). BO all stitches.
